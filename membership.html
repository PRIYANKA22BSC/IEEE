ENTREPRENEUR

Owner, Founder, Co-founder, or controlling shareholder of a company with a gross turnover above 20 Cr INR/ 2.5 million USD with over 50 employees.

PROFESSIONAL

President, Chairman of the Board, Managing Partner, or senior Partner (or equivalent) at a qualifying company.

  • with an annual income/compensation above 1 Cr INR/ 130,000 USD.
  • Qualifying company must have a minimum turnover of 200 Cr INR/ 27 million USD, with over 150 employees.

YOUNG LEADER

Individuals ranging from the age of 18 to 30 years.

  • Entrepreneurs:Owner, founder, or co-founder of a company with an annual turnover above 5 Cr INR/ 600,000 USD and 15+ employees.
  • Professional: Young achievers with an annual income/compensation of 50 lakhs INR/ 60,000 USD.

MEMBERSHIP PROCESS

Application Request

STEP 1

DLC, is an online ‘Invite-Only' membership club, however, any ordinary people is welcome to apply by filling the application form. The application form will be reviewed by the membership committee to check if it meets the DLC membership criteria.
Any application endorsed or referred by a DLC member will have higher weightage.

Application Review

STEP 2

Once the Application is accepted, the membership committee will schedule a preliminary interview with the applicant. The team will also apprise the applicant, of the DLC offerings, benefits to the applicant & their firm as well as the membership processes till the final membership.

Application Approval

STEP 3

Application is approved by the anonymous membership committee in every chapter, post the approval, the payment link and the contract are shared with the applicant.

Membership fees

STEP 4

DLC Annual Membership fee is 4000 USD. The membership fees are all-inclusive with no extra charge for Events, Global Summits, etc. for member, their spouse/partner and children. DLC Retreats are the only offering that would come at an additional cost to members.

GLOBAL FAMILY MEMBERSHIP

Upon acceptance into DLC as per the membership qualifying criteria, the member’s immediate family, that is spouse / partner & children ​(young adult up to 25 years), get to avail the same offerings at any DLC chapter globally.​​

CORPORATE MEMBERSHIP

Any Corporate with minimum turnover of 5 million USD and 200+ employees in Manufacturing / Equivalent can nominate its Shareholder / Management / C-Suite or the entire top management for DLC membership. These memberships are non-transferable & also include the nominee member’s & family for 12 months.​
