Membership

At M.O.D.I. Forum, we offer exclusive membership opportunities designed to connect leaders, investors, and visionaries from around the globe. Our members gain access to a network of like-minded professionals, cutting-edge insights, and high-value opportunities that drive growth, innovation, and collaboration.

Business Visionary

The individual who is a Founder, Co-Founder, Owner, or Major Stakeholder in a company.

  • Annual Revenue: Must have a company with an annual revenue exceeding 15 Crore INR (1.8 million USD).
  • Company Size: The business should employ more than 40 people, demonstrating significant growth and market presence.

Senior Executives

Senior leaders like CEOs, MDs, or Principal Partners drive strategy and decision-making in top companies.

  • Income/Compensation: Must have an annual income or compensation exceeding 80 Lakh INR (100,000 USD).
  • Qualifying Company: Must have an annual turnover of at least 150 Crore INR (19 million USD) and employ over 100 people.

Emerging Leaders

Individuals ranging from the age of 18 to 30 years.

  • Entrepreneurs:Founders, co-founders, or owners of businesses with annual revenue over 3 Crore INR (400,000 USD) and 10+ employees.
  • Professional: High-achieving professionals earning at least 30 Lakh INR (40,000 USD) annually.
